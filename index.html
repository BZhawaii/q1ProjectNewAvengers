New Avengers

Have you ever wondered why the Avengers had so many weaker heroes, like Black Widow and Hawkeye? The team is supposed to be fighting intergalactic threats and defending the planet from the most powerful villains. The results do speak for themselves. However, we have all wondered what the Avengers would be like if we could hand pick the heroes that were on the team. Well, this is your opportunity to do just that. On this page you will have the opportunity to name your team of Avengers and then to pick each of the individual heroes that will make up your awesome new team. Afterwards, you can go to the next page and see your awesome new team. Go New Avengers!

Instructions

Please enter the name of your Avengers team. Then go down the page to the work board to pick your individual heroes. Fill every role with one hero and change it as much as you like.

Leader

This is the ultimate leader of the team and there is only one, so choose wisely. The leader must have the intelligence to strategize, both long term and on the fly. The leader must also possess strong leadership skills and be able to get his or her troops to follow them into battle. The leader should also be personable enough to make the team want to work under them. The leader is the make or break cog on a team so this should be your best choice.

Co-leaders

The leader is the most indispensible member of the team, which means that there must be viable options to take over in case of emergencies. There are two co-leaders, and it is their duty to take over for a fallen leader, but also to lead smaller divisions of the team. With a leader and two co-leaders, the team can easily split into three segments and still excell in carrying out their plans. Having two co-leaders will also help with rotations and ensure that the team is never without a leader. These positions should be filled with great care.

Rank and File

The Rank and File Members will simply be referred to as members. There will be a total of four positions to be filled for the team. With such a wide variety of heroes to choose from, this is the area of picking a team, where you can define your team. It's possible to simply pick just the strongest heroes, but that doesn't provide for a good balance. It is here where you will realize the need for some of the current members of the Avengers. As you pick your members, think about how their personalities and powers will work within the team that you envision.
